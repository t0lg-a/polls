Trump Approval Polling Average

RacetotheWH is tracking the latest Donald Trump approval rating polls. It will be updated every day with the latest polling.

To improve accuracy, the polling average gives more weight to polls:

1. Published by Pollsters that have a Strong Track Record of Accuracy
2. That Interviewed a Large Sample of Voters
3. That Were Conducted Recently

The Polling Average Now Includes a Second Option! Explore how many voters Strongly/Somewhat Approve and Disapprove of Trump. If we’re missing any polls, please feel free to let us know by emailing Logan Phillips, the RacetotheWH founder, at racetothewh@gmail.com. While you’re at RacetotheWH, be sure to explore the latest polls across all races, or dive into our polling averages for the Senate, Governor Races, the 2028 Dem and GOP Presidential Primaries, and the Generic Ballot.
