2026 Governor Polling

Track the latest polls for the 2026 Governor Elections. This page is split into three sections. Start with the general election: choose a Governor race, pick the candidates representing each party, and see who leads in the polling. Second, scroll to the primaries section to see which candidates are leading in the Democratic and Republican contests that will decide each party’s nominees. Finally, the third section tracks how popular all the potential candidates are in each state using favorability/unfavourability polling. To provide a more accurate picture of the election, our polling average gives more weight to polls from pollsters with an excellent track record, polls conducted recently, and polls with large sample sizes.

If we’re missing any polls, please feel free to let us know by emailing Logan Phillips, the RacetotheWH founder, at racetothewh@gmail.com. While you’re at RacetotheWH, be sure to explore our polling for the Senate, the 2028 Dem and GOP Presidential Primaries, and our interactive predictions for Governor, House and Senate elections.
